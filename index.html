About Us

The tale of the legendary University House (UH) dates back to 1919 when it was opened in Gardens, near the Hiddingh Campus. Its name was bestowed upon it by the original warden Professor Lawrence Crawford. Over the years UH has proved to resilient despite a number of temporary closures and relocations. UH no longer resides in this location - as it was closed in 1937 and is now the site of Gardens Commercial High School. The residence was then moved to lower campus where it was again eventually closed to accomodate the opening of the UCT Graduate School of Business. After such a storied past it is now located (as it has been since 1958) on 1 Matopo Road in a structure originally built as a temporary housing for WWII servicemen in 1946. The location on Matopo Road was originally intended to house graduate and more mature students but is now of UCT's first teir residences. FRESHERS: WHAT TO BRING

The things you absolutely MUST have with you on arrival:

1. Two padlocks (34mm in diameter)
2. 2x Passport Photos
3. Proof of Residence Acceptance
4. Proof of Identification
5. Your emergency contact details as well as that of your General Practitioner.
6. Your medical aid details
7. Toiletries
8. Washing powder ( we do have a washing facility at res equipped with washing machines and tumble dryers)
9. Pillow
10. Linen (Bed linen and shower towel)
11. Hangers

So you already have everything you need but we want your room to feel like a home which reflects your personality. So here are a few things you might WANT to bring with you, some of which you should buy in Cape Town to avoid being over-weight.

1. Formal attire (We as a res have several formal events throughout the year)
2. Cultural wear ( For our heritage day and various other celebrations)
3. A swimming costume ( we do have access to a swimming pool and a huge ocean)
4. Desk lamp
5. Musical instrument
6. Cups and Glasses (For drinks)
7. Cutlery
8. Fan heater
9. Small bar fridge
10. Posters and Pictures
